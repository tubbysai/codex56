How to play

Number cards score their face value. Aces score 1, Jacks and Queens 10, Kings 0, and Jokers −2. Three matching ranks in a horizontal row or vertical column are removed and score 0.

Once either grid is fully revealed, the other player gets one last turn. The lowest total wins.
