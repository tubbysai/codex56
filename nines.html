Single player card game

Keep it low.

Nine cards. Three rows. A little planning, a little nerve. Trade cards to build matching rows and finish with fewer points than Marlow.

  • Begin by revealing any two of your cards.
  • On each turn, take the top discard or draw a mystery card, then replace one card in your grid.
  • Three equal cards in a horizontal row score zero.

How to play

Number cards score their face value. Aces score 1, Jacks and Queens 10, Kings 0, and Jokers −2. A horizontal row of three matching ranks scores 0.

Once either grid is fully revealed, the other player gets one last turn. The lowest total wins.
